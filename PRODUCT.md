# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, founders, product teams, recruiters, and collaborators evaluating Segun as a frontend engineer. They arrive with little time and need fast evidence of taste, execution quality, and practical web-building range.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio introduces Segun, demonstrates frontend craft through selected work, and converts interest into contact. Success means visitors quickly understand what he builds, trust that he can ship polished interfaces, and have a clear path to inspect projects or reach out.

## Brand Personality

Precise, kinetic, assured. The brand should feel like a focused engineer with visual taste: sharp enough for product work, expressive enough to be remembered.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic teal developer portfolios, centered SaaS hero templates, oversized icon-card grids, decorative gradient text, and vague claims without proof. The site should not feel like a theme demo or a static resume page.

## Design Principles

- Lead with proof, not decoration.
- Make the work inspectable in the first pass.
- Use motion as evidence of frontend care, never as delay.
- Keep copy direct, confident, and human.
- Preserve fast contact paths across device sizes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible links and controls, legible type at small sizes, and a reduced-motion experience that keeps content fully available.
